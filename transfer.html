Important information about the appeal process

1. Review Your Degree Audit

Start with a careful review of your degree audit to see how your courses and credits transferred. The report shows what requirements you've met and what you still need to meet. Go to Degree Audit

2. Request a Review of Your Transfer Records

After you've reviewed the audit, you can request a review if you have questions. You will need to choose which course(s) you want reviewed and complete all information in the "Add Request" form.

Have a question?

Contact your transfer specialist at Transfer.Evaluations@metrostate.edu.
